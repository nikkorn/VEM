Inventory
=========

Each player has an inventory made up of 15 slots.

Any slot can be made the active slot, The players 'use' action will use the active item.

Items do not stack.


Container
=========

A container has a number of slots.

Better ones have more slots.

Containers are attached to placements. E.g. A tilled earth placement could have 3 slots (for producing 1-3 crops)
While a 'Chest' placement may have 30 slots.

Items do not stack.

Containers can be opened/closed by a player. Only one player can interact with a container at one time.
